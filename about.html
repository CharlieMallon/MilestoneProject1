About Me

I originate from the beautiful Lake District, UK and have been lucky enough for my work to take me all over the world, sometimes for months at a time. Growing up I watched my parents run multiple businesses, this has instilled a strong work ethic and drive to succeed from a young age.


There was a time when I was so focussed on my work, I took little care of my mind and body, didn’t learn how to balance my life. This resulted in multiple burnouts. Now, after several years of personal development, reflection, training and trial and error, I have created a lifestyle that ensures I can run my business, spend time with my family and friends, enjoy a range of activities and hobbies and successfully maintain a healthy mind and body.

My mission is Helping High Achieving Professionals to find self confidence, balance and happiness throughout their lives, whilst still achieving everything they want to in their successful career and life. You CAN have success in both.
