About Me

Originating from and predominantly living and working in the beautiful Lake District, UK. However, I have been lucky enough for my work to take me all over the world, sometimes for months at a time. Growing up watching my parents run multiple businesses instilled a strong work ethic and drive to succeed from a young age. This carried through into my work life. I really enjoy work so much so I kept taking on more – for a whole year I had 6 different jobs!

My mission is Helping High Achieving Professionals to find self confidence, balance and happiness throughout their lives, whilst still achieving everything they want to in their successful career and life. You CAN have success in both.
