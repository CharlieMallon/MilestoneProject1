About Me

Originating from and predominantly living and working in the beautiful Lake District, UK. However, I have been lucky enough for my work to take me all over the world, sometimes for months at a time. Growing up watching my parents run multiple businesses instilled a strong work ethic and drive to succeed from a young age. This carried through into my work life. I really enjoy work so much so I kept taking on more – for a whole year I had 6 different jobs! When I found myself working every hour available in a career I enjoyed, With responsibility came the expectations, harder to switch off, loss of confidence and eventually anxiety. Days off used to terrify me – what do people do with them? This started to seep into my work and removed all the enjoyment and no time to see friends, family and just have a life! From the outside I was still successful, I just no longer felt it. Then, It all came crashing down around me. I realised I had to change some things. Consequently I developed boundaries, confidence, stamina and a healthier, stronger mindset to rediscover the joy in life without compromising my work. I achieve everything in work that I want to plus have time for the people and activities that make me happy. I have learnt that all of this can be achieved as well as letting your body rest and recover.

My mission is Helping High Achieving Professionals to find self confidence, balance and happiness throughout their lives, whilst still achieving everything they want to in their successful career and life. You CAN have success in both.

I have gone from ...

  • Working and being promoted in a job which i no longer enjoyed
  • Having only 1 day off work a month - if i was lucky
  • Never getting to see my family and friends
  • Feeling anxious, alone and lost on days off
  • Living in a state of constant burnout, stress and overwhelm
  • Achieving all work goals but no personal goals. So focused on the next goal, I often overlooked the goals and everything I had already achieved. Some goals felt empty when I achieved them.
  • Being so busy working, having very little time to do basic things like eat and have time to myself

Then, It all came crashing down around me. I realised I had to change some things.

To ...

  • Having a business which i love
  • Seeing my family and friends regularly, I can now be there for them when they need me.
  • Have boundaries in place which allow me to work efficiently and effectively and still enjoy my evenings and days off doing things that make me happy.
  • I have learnt to relax and have passions outside of work.
  • Becoming a number 1 bestselling author.
  • Most importantly, I am Confident, Happy, Healthy and Unstoppable!
  • Feeling energised
